bertha is an inhabitant of the vast land of shapia. they are a humble present being raised by their single mother. 
they've always felt ostracisized due to being a half-dragon in a world of shape-humans.

one day, the evil, long sealed dragon king is broken out of his realm. consolidating his forces, the dragon strikes shapia and kidnaps the noble king.
with much of shapia under the dragon king's control, bertha takes it upon themselves to make their way to the dragon king's castle and save the king

bertha is actually the child of the dragon king, the illegitimate child of the dragon king and the now exiled queen.
the king has been corrupted in the grasp of the dragon king.
